Bombay Sandwich

About

The Bombay Sandwich is a beloved Mumbai street food that’s as colorful and lively as the city itself. It’s a delicious medley of fresh, crunchy vegetables like Tomato, Onions, Cucumber, Potatoes, Beetroot, layered generously with tangy green chutney, sweet and spicy sauces, and a sprinkle of chaat masala that gives it that signature zing. All of this is sandwiched between soft, buttered bread slices toasted to perfection. Every bite delivers a perfect harmony of textures and flavors — spicy, tangy, sweet, and savory — making it a must-try snack that captures the vibrant spirit and bold tastes of Mumbai’s streets. Whether you enjoy it as a quick snack or a light meal, the Bombay Sandwich is comfort food with a flavorful punch!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Vegetables-Tomato, Onions, Cucumber, Potatoes, Beetroot.
  • Cheese slices
  • Butter
  • Green Chutney (click for recipe)
  • Chaat masala

Steps

  1. Boil Potatoes
  2. Thinly slice all the vegetables
  3. Take 2 slices of bread
  4. Apply butter on both sides of each bread
  5. Apply the mint green chutney on both sides of each bread
  6. Take one of the breads
  7. Put the sliced vegetables on top of each other on the bread
  8. Sprinkle chaat masala on the vegetables
  9. Put a cheese slice on top of the vegetable slices
  10. Close the sandwich with the other bread
  11. Apply some butter on both sides of the sandwich
  12. Heat the griller or a pan and put the sandwich on it
  13. Let the sandwich grill for 4/5 minutes on both sides
  14. After this remove it and cut it in half and best enjoyed with the mint green chutney and sprinkling some yellow shev on!!
